Why Life on Land Matters?

Land ecosystems are the foundation of life. From lush forests to vast grasslands, these environments provide habitat for millions of species, regulate climate, and support human survival. However, rapid deforestation, habitat destruction, and climate change threaten biodiversity and natural ecosystems.
